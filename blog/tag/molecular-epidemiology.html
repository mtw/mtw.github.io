May 29 2024 Xinyang flavivirus is a novel tick-borne Orthoflavivirus

In this publication, we describe the discovery of Xinyang flavivirus (XiFV), a novel tick-borne virus isolated from Haemaphysalis flava ticks in China. Phylogenetic analyses indicate that XiFV is closely related to other tick-only flaviviruses, such as Mpulungu flavivirus (MPFV) from Zambia and Ngoye virus (NGOV) from Senegal. This relationship places XiFV within a unique clade of orthoflaviviruses that appear to infect only ticks, without evidence of vertebrate infection
