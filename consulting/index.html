Home » RNA Forecast Consulting Services

Are you navigating the complexities of RNA computational biology and bioinformatics in your pharmaceutical or biotechnology venture? Do you want to sharpen your skills in in silico RNA structure prediction or need expert help designing RNA for breakthrough applications like mRNA vaccines?

At RNA Forecast, we partner exclusively with industry leaders to deliver hands-on, results-driven consultancy in computational and structural RNA biology. Led by Michael T. Wolfinger, Principal Investigator at the University of Vienna and founder of RNA Forecast, our team combines academic rigor with real-world impact.

Our Services

Custom RNA Structure Prediction

  • In silico modeling of secondary and tertiary folds
  • Tailored workflows using the ViennaRNA Package and ViennaRNA Python interface
  • Benchmarking against known reference data for maximum confidence

RNA Design & Optimization

  • Custom ncRNA and mRNA design
  • Folding pathway optimization through kinetic modeling
  • Advanced incorporation of modified nucleotides for enhanced function and stability

Training & Workshops

  • Onsite or virtual courses in RNA structure prediction (from beginner to expert)
  • Step-by-step tutorials on ViennaRNA command-line usage, and Python API integration
  • Hands-on exercises for Windows, Mac, and Linux users

Data Analysis & Integration

  • SHAPE/DMS-guided and high-throughput chemical probing pipelines for RNA structure prediction
  • Custom scripts for complex RNA structure analysis
  • Integration of experimental data into computational models to reveal real-world folding behaviors

How We Work

1. Discovery call
